Kim Seokjin of BTS

About



Kim Seok Jin (김석진), better known by his stage name Jin, is the vocalist and visual of the phenomenal boy group BTS of Big Hit Entertainment. Jin was just walking down a street when he was spotted by a casting agent and was invited to audition for Big Hit Entertainment. With a head-turner presence, sweet vocals, and acting education, Jin became a trainee alongside his fellow members. Eventually, Jin debuted with the group and went on to become one of the most famous artists of his generation.

Famous Songs


Epiphany

Epiphany is a song by Jin of BTS. It was released on August, 9, 2018 as a comeback trailer and appears as the thirteenth track of CD A in their second compilation album Love Yourself: Answer.

Listen to Epiphany by Jin

Moon

Moon is a song by Jin of BTS. It was released on February 21, 2020, and appears as the sixteenth track in their fourth studio album Map of the Soul: 7. The song also appears as the fourth track on CD2 of BTS's first anthology album Proof.

Listen to Moon by Jin

Awake

Awake is a solo song by Jin of BTS. It was released on October 10, 2016, and appears as the ninth track for their second studio album Wings and the repackage album You Never Walk Alone. A christmas version was released on December 4, 2016 as a gift for his birthday.

Listen to Awake by Jin
